I am an audio engineer working in the Netherlands. Beginning in 2017 in my birthtown of Bergen op Zoom, I have recorded, mixed, and mastered acoustic, film, and classical music from jazz, pop, and rock, to heavy metal.

About

In 2017 Cees Schnellink gave me a chance to assist him and his engineers at Studio-T.

In 2019 I started studying music production and audio design at the University of arts in Utrecht (Hogeschool van de kunsten Utrecht), which I'm still associated with.

In 2023 - 2024 I set up Third Place together with Jeremy Leung, whom I met during my studying years.

Third place for me was a pilot to make promotional content for young musicians, released under our YouTube channel.

Ever since Cees gave me the chance of starting out in this field, I've been trying to repay those chances.

I aim to help aspiring musicians and to create space for audio designers to discover and develop their talents.

I wish to build work environments where engineers can collaborate in an "as a team, for the team" structure rather than a competitive hierarchic structure to promote professional and personal growth.
